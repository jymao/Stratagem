Stratagem

A turn-based strategy role-playing game in which the player makes a team from six unique units to face off against an enemy team.


How to Play

Mouse for controls.

Deployment Phase: Choose a unit for each location.

Player Phase: Units can move and attack once per turn. End turn for enemy phase.

Stats: Attack applies to defense, and magic applies to resist. Speed determines who attacks first in a fight.
